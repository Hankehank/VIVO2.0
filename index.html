About Us

FoodShare Malaysia is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste and helping communities in need. We connect donors, volunteers, and local organizations to make a positive impact across Malaysia.

Our Services

Food Donation

Facilitating food donations from businesses and individuals to support those in need.

Food Rescue

Collecting surplus food from restaurants and stores to reduce waste and feed communities.

Community Support

Partnering with NGOs and volunteers to strengthen sustainable food-sharing networks.
