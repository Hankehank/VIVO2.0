About Us

Connecting communities through food sharing and reducing food waste.

Our Mission

VIVO 2.0 is dedicated to creating an interactive platform that connects individuals and communities to share surplus food efficiently. By leveraging technology and community initiatives, we aim to fight hunger, reduce waste, and promote sustainability across Malaysia.

Our platform allows users to donate, track, and receive food donations, making the process transparent and convenient for everyone involved.
